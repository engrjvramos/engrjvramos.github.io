01 About

something about myself.

Hello, my name is Jose Ramos.

I’m a self-taught web developer & programmer who loves building high quality & responsive websites and web apps.

I graduated in 2017 from Technological Institute of the Philippines with a BS in Civil Engineering. I've had the privilege of working with my father's contracting business and with a huge corporation.

My interest in coding started in 2021 due to my curiosity in building websites and mobile apps. I took a leap and studied modern web development on Codecademy and Udemy. I quickly absorbed new concepts, while bringing a positive attitude to every challenge.
