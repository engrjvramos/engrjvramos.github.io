About Me

Hello, my name is Jose Ramos.

I’m a self-taught web developer & programmer who loves building high quality & responsive websites and web apps. Writing clean code & designing user interfaces with attention to detail matters to me.

I graduated in 2017 from Technological Institute of the Philippines with a BS in Civil Engineering. I've had the privilege of working with my father's contracting business and with a huge corporation. My background pretty much revolved around the construction industry.

My interest in web development started in 2021 due to my curiosity in building websites and mobile apps. I was already good at math and programming due to my profession so I decided to try and learn HTML and CSS! After hours of studying, coding & building various projects, I realized that being a software developer was my true calling and so I decided to pursue and shift my career in the tech industry.
